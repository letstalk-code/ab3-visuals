A Photographer's Precision. A Filmmaker's Vision.

Anthony Bove III · Founder

Bringing a Cinematographer's Eye to Every Property.

Anthony Bove III founded AB3 Visuals with a single mission: make every property look as remarkable as it deserves to. With a background spanning commercial film, event coverage, and architectural photography, Anthony brings a rare combination of technical expertise and creative instinct to every shoot.

Based in St. Petersburg, FL, AB3 Visuals now serves agents, brokers, developers, and brands across the entire Tampa Bay area — from intimate condos to waterfront estates to national television commercials.
